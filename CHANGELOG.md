## February 8, 2026

**February 5, 2026 Skill Balance Update**

- Updated skill data for 97 skills across all 10 professions from the [February 5, 2026 game balance patch](https://wiki.guildwars.com/wiki/Feedback:Game_updates/20260205)
- Nature rituals received significant buffs: faster activation (5s → 3s) and longer durations
- Many underused skills got energy cost reductions, faster cast times, and better scaling

## January 20, 2026

- Fixed incorrect weapon IDs and attribute mappings for wands
- Fixed incorrect insignia IDs for armor
- Updated DB to reflect the fixed IDs (to build authors: please double-check the armor in your builds)
- Fixed collaborator search dropdown being cut off at edge of modal
- Build previews in search results are now proper links (cmd/ctrl+click to open in new tab)
- Star builds directly from spotlight search without leaving the modal
- Click on usernames to view their public profile and builds

## January 16, 2026

- Hover over skills in the picker to preview their full details
- Equipment now validates against your profession — mismatched runes and insignias are flagged
- Added PvE-only weapon upgrades like "Of the Warrior" suffix mods
- Fixed PvE-only skills not appearing in the skill picker
- Fixed incorrect data for some runes and insignias
- Faster build search results
- Fixed an issue when creating builds without being logged in
- Improved modal accessibility and behavior across the app

## January 15, 2026

**Builder V2**

- Add equipment codes and configure individual armor, weapons, runes, and insignias
- Pick individual skills with search - see costs, recharge, and attribute-scaled descriptions
- Build variants let you save alternative skill bar setups (anti-caster, budget, etc.)
- Team builds now support up to 12 players with duplicate player slots
- Create new builds from existing ones as templates
- Collaborators can now edit your builds with you
- Private builds stay hidden from search but shareable via direct link
- Share draft builds before publishing
- Create builds without logging in first - sign up when you're ready to save
- Discord and email login options added
- Spotlight search (Cmd+K / Ctrl+K) searches both user builds and 26,000+ PvX Wiki archives
- Submit feedback, bug reports and feature suggestions via the feedback button

## January 12, 2026

- Notes editor now shows a live character count (and fixed a bug where character limits were counting JSON bytes instead of actual text)
- Nested lists, blockquotes, and dividers now render correctly in build notes
- Improved search experience on mobile devices

## January 11, 2026

- Search for builds by name, profession, or skills
